Yettel Market Pulse · what it is
Know what moved before anyone tells you

You're the CEO of a top-3 CEE telecom. Markets move overnight — by the time something reaches you as a formal briefing, the raw signal is gone. You give it a market and a time window — "Hungary, last 7 days" — and it scans three signal layers, read through your strategic lens.

🏁 External
Competitor pricing, network announcements, exec moves, press — live from the web.
⚖️ Regulatory
Spectrum decisions, competition rulings, consultations — flagged & interpreted.
📧 Internal
What your own team is already flagging in email — before you'd have seen it.
🎯
It asks before it runs. No market + time window? It stops and asks — "CEE, 30 days" is a different brief from "Hungary, 7 days." The discipline is the value.

What good looks like — the brief
  • Headline — the single most important thing that moved.
  • What Moved — 3-5 developments: named actor, what, when, cited source.
  • What It Means For Us — tied to your 2026 priorities, by number.
  • What To Watch Next — 1-2 things in the next 7-14 days.
  • Internal Signals (Stage 2+) — what your team already flagged.
✨
The wow: "What It Means For Us" — through your lens, not a generic news summary.
