Yettel Market Pulse · your persona
Same CEO — a different problem

You're the same CEO of a top-3 CEE telecom. But markets move overnight — competitors announce, regulators signal, your own team flags issues by email. By the time something reaches you as a formal briefing, it's been filtered and the raw signal is gone. Market Pulse gives you that raw signal, through your strategic lens, before anyone else has filtered it.

Role
Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
Markets
Hungary & Bulgaria (top-3 CEE operator)
Competitors in play
Magyar Telekom · 4iG · A1 · Vivacom · DIGI
Reports to
Group CEO (Marcus Weber) · Group Board
The job
Know what moved in your market — before anyone tells you — and what it means for your 2026 priorities.
Market Pulse · what the agent does
Know what moved before anyone tells you

You give it a market and a time window — "Hungary, last 7 days" — and it scans three signal layers, read through your strategic lens, and returns a structured brief.

🏁 External
Competitor pricing, network announcements, exec moves, press — live from the web.
⚖️ Regulatory
Spectrum decisions, competition rulings, consultations — flagged & interpreted.
📧 Internal
What your own team is already flagging in email — before you'd have seen it.
🎯
It asks before it runs. No market + time window? It stops and asks — "CEE, 30 days" is a different brief from "Hungary, 7 days." The discipline is the value.

Market Pulse · what good looks like
One brief. Four sections.
📄 The brief structure
  • Headline — the single most important thing that moved.
  • What Moved — 3-5 developments: named actor, what, when, cited source.
  • What It Means For Us — tied to your 2026 priorities, by number.
  • What To Watch Next — 1-2 things in the next 7-14 days. Early warning.
  • Internal Signals (Stage 2+) — what your team already flagged.
Three build stages
Basic
Web + files · you give market + window · returns the 4-section brief. External signals only.
Intermediate
Work IQ email connected · adds the Internal Signals section · brief auto-emailed.
Advanced
Runs at 9:00 AM daily · last 24h · Hungary + Bulgaria · no input needed.
✨
The wow: "What It Means For Us" — the external signal through your lens, not a generic news summary.
